Getting here

City Hostel is a short walk from Pike Place Market, Olympic Sculpture Park, and the Elliott Bay Trail, and is situated right across the street from some of Belltown’s most colorful bars and restaurants. Explore the area and enjoy some of Seattle’s most famous sites!

from baggage claim, follow the signs to the LINK LIGHT RAIL (10 minute walk).
Pay $3 per ticket (purchase before entering train) to Westlake Station (downtown). Take the 3rd Ave & Pine St exit, turn left and walk 1 block to 2nd Ave (towards the water). Turn right onto 2nd Ave, walk to 2327 2nd Ave (10 minute walk)! A Taxi will cost approx $45, or try the following shuttle services: www.shuttleexpress.com www.downtownairporter.com (to major hotels downtown with a short walk to the hostel)
