Welcome to City Hostel


City Hostel is located in the historic Lorraine Hotel building in the Belltown neighborhood of Seattle. Part hostel and part art gallery, each room in City Hostel has been turned into a canvas by local artists like Hyperreal, Weirdo, and Anika Wilson.

Getting here

City Hostel is a short walk from Pike Place Market, Olympic Sculpture Park, and the Elliott Bay Trail, and is situated right across the street from some of Belltown’s most colorful bars and restaurants. Explore the area and enjoy some of Seattle’s most famous sites!
